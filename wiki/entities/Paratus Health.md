## Overview

Paratus Health is an SF Bay Area startup that builds autonomous agents designed to entirely replace clinic staff. The company's agents handle patient intake, insurance verification, documentation, and billing preparation, integrating deeply into electronic health records (EHRs) like Epic without requiring fragmented tools.
