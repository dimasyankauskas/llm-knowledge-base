## Overview

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company based in San Francisco. In the CUA domain, OpenAI introduced its Operator product, powered by a specialized Computer-Using Agent model that merges GPT-4o multimodal vision with reinforcement learning architectures.

## Contributions

- **Operator (CUA model)**: A specialized agent that natively interprets buttons, menus, and text fields purely from visual input, executing cross-application workflows and orchestrating complex tasks across the operating system layer
- **GPT-4o vision integration**: The CUA model leverages GPT-4o's advanced multimodal vision capabilities combined with rigorous reinforcement learning
- **OS-level orchestration**: Operator acts as an autonomous user, capable of executing complex workflows across applications at the operating system level
